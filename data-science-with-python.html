    Frequently Asked Questions

    Below are some of the frequently asked questions to us

    • What is the duration of the Foundations of Data Science with Python Program ?

      This program is for 35days of Training and then 1 week of Exploratory Data Analysis Project.

    • What is the mode of Delivery for this Program?

      Foundations Data Science with Python program is a complete Offline Program in Nagpur.

    • What is structure of the Foundations Data Science with Python program?

      The program is structured to be more of practical than theory. So there will be 9:1 ratio of practical is to theory sessions for all the 4-5 weeks of duraiton.

    • Will I receive any certificate at the end of the course?

      Yes, Foundations Data Science with Python program is a Certification Course.

    • Do I need to bring my own laptop?

      The candidates need to bring their own laptops, the technology requirement shall be shared at the time of enrollment.

    • How can I apply for this course ?

      If you are interested in the Program, you can apply through the online application form. Please reach out to us at +91 93250 13467, +91 72766 45484 for any program or admission related queries.

    • Will there be any Placement or Internship Assistance ?

      No,This is not a placement driven program but Yes we do provide internship opportunity at the end of this program.

    • What is the refund policy of the course ?

      We advise all candidates to have complete information before enrolling in the program. No refund requests will be accepted once the fee has been paid. However, there is a deferment policy where a candidate can choose to defer his enrollment to a future batch.

    • I am a First year student of my graduation, am I eligible to do this course ?

      Yes as this course is from complete scratch so a guy who is in 10th or 12th standard and has no coding experience as well can do this course.
